    FIFA Online 4 is a free-to-play massively multiplayer online football developed by EA Spearhead and published by Nexon. It was announced on 2 November 2017,and released on 17 May 2018 in South Korea. It is released in various Southeast Asia countries before the FIFA World Cup on 14 June. No date has been revealed for an English version yet
